OUR STORY

Kids Writing Consult is a pioneering initiative based in Accra, Ghana, that champions the art of storytelling among children and young adults. It serves as a creative platform where budding writers, particularly students, can nurture their imaginations, develop their writing skills, and see their stories transformed into professionally published storybooks.



Mission | Vision | Values

At Kids Writing Consult, our mission is to inspire and empower children and young adults in Accra, Ghana, by nurturing their storytelling talents. We provide a creative platform where budding writers can develop their imaginations, enhance their writing skills, and transform their stories into professionally published storybooks. Through this, we aim to cultivate a love for literacy, creativity, and self-expression.
